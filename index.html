Single-Phase CFD of a VVER-1200 Rod Bundle

Conjugate heat transfer simulation of a 19-rod hexagonal mini-bundle (VVER-1200) over the bottom 1/10 of the active core (~1.8M cells, SST k-ω γ-Reθ). CFD matched analytical references to 0.18% on peak fuel temperature, 1.9% on coolant ΔT, and 8.1% on pressure drop.

Star-CCM+ CHT SST k-ω γ-Reθ Hexagonal Bundle
⚛️

CFD Simulation of a 3×3 PWR Rod Bundle

Full conjugate heat transfer simulation of a 3×3 PWR fuel rod bundle. Non-uniform radial power distribution (up to 2.51×10⁸ W/m³) cross-validated between ANSYS Fluent (1/4-symmetry) and Star-CCM+ (full geometry) against Petukhov & Dittus-Boelter correlations.

ANSYS Fluent Star-CCM+ CHT k-ω SST
💧

Flow Analysis in a PWR Fuel Rod Subchannel

Turbulent coolant flow in a 3.66 m bare-rod subchannel (D_h = 11.78 mm). Two independent CFD codes converged to within ~1% of each other and of the Darcy-Weisbach / Filonenko analytical reference.

RANS k-ε k-ω SST Darcy-Weisbach

Steady-State Heat Conduction in a TRISO Particle

3D Star-CCM+ model of a 5-layer TRISO fuel particle (2 GW/m³ source) validated against 1D analytical solution (T_max = 1106.35 K). Mesh error < 0.0084% across all refinement levels.

Star-CCM+ Spherical Conduction Mesh Independence UO₂
🔬 Numerical Methods & Heat Transfer · Python / Fortran

⚛️

IAEA-3D PWR Benchmark · Neutron Diffusion Solver

From-scratch two-group neutron diffusion solver for the 3-D IAEA PWR core benchmark (ANL-7416). Mesh-centered FDM with harmonic-mean interface coefficients, Robin vacuum BCs, power iteration + red-black SOR. Reproduces the reference k_eff = 1.02903 to within −7.9 pcm and matches the 1977 VENTURE mesh-convergence sequence.

Fortran OpenMP Finite Difference Eigenvalue Red-Black SOR

U-238 Decay Chain Modeling

14-stage decay chain (U-238 → Pb-206) with half-lives spanning 10⁹ years to seconds — a severely stiff system. Generic Implicit Euler solver in Fortran, mirroring legacy HPC nuclear-code logic.

Fortran 90 Implicit Euler Stiff ODEs
